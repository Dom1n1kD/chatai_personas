How to use the KITE Persona Generator

Choose a pre-defined persona or build your own — then launch the conversation directly in Chat AI.

  1. 1 Choose a persona: Select Sarah, Jonas, or Viki from the dropdown, or pick Custom Persona to write your own name, description, and info message.
  2. 2 Adjust the Temperature: Controls how the AI responds. Closer to 0 means focused and predictable; closer to 1 means more varied and spontaneous. For realistic personas, values between 0.7 and 0.9 work well.
  3. 3 Click "Generate Persona": This creates a unique Chat AI link with your persona's settings baked in.
  4. 4 Start the chat: Click "Chat with [Name]" to open directly in Chat AI, or use "Copy URL" to share the link with someone else.
